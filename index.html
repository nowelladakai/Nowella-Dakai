ABOUT US

Our Story

Discover the heart and soul of Musket Cove Island Resort

SINCE 1994

A Legacy of Hospitality

Musket Cove Island Resort began as a vision to create a sanctuary where luxury meets nature. Founded in 1994, our resort has grown from a small island retreat to one of Fiji's most celebrated destinations.

Our commitment to sustainable tourism and authentic Fijian hospitality has earned us recognition from travelers worldwide. We take pride in preserving the natural beauty of our island while providing world-class amenities.

Today, we continue to welcome guests from around the globe, sharing our piece of paradise with those seeking adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable memories.
